Pizza neapolitan recipe

Recipe description:
Dough ingredients:
Ingredients Amount
  • Flour
400 gramms
  • Salt
10 gramms
  • Dried yeast
1-3 gramms
Sauce ingredients:
Ingredients Amount
  • Tomato Sauce
150 - 250 ml
Cooking steps:
  1. Start making the dough by mixing the listing ingredients.
  2. Knead the dough for 10 minutes approximatelly.
  3. Let the dough rise by resting it for at least 3 hours, or overnight.
  4. Preheat the overn to 230 Â°C.
  5. Make a circular shape out of the dough using a rolling pin.
  6. Put the sauce on the pasta using a spoon.
  7. Lay your desired toppings on top of the pizza
  8. Cook the pizza in the preheated oven for 13 minutes, or until the bottom of the dough gets brown.
