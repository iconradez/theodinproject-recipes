Cacio-e-pepe recipe

Recipe description:
Main ingredients:
Ingredients Amount
  • Pasta (in italian:cacio)
170 gramms
  • Cheese (in italian:cacio)
200 gramms
  • Peper (in italian:pepe)
1 tablespoon cracked black pepper
Cooking steps:
  1. Put the pasta in boiling water and cook al dente.
  2. With the pasta water mix the cheese to make the sauce and season it with pepper generously.
  3. Put the pasta into a pan on low-medium heat while mixing in the sauce.
  4. Once the sauce covers the pasta serve it is ready to be served.
  5. Additionally add cheese or pepper on top of it.
