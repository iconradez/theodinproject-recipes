Trofie al pesto recipe

Recipe description:
Main ingredients:
Ingredients Amount
  • Trofie pasta
200 gramms
  • Salt
1 teaspoon
  • Pesto sauce
150 ml
Cooking steps:
  1. Put the pasta in boiling water and cook al dente.
  2. Put the pasta into a pan on low-medium heat while mixing in the sauce.
  3. Once the sauce covers the pasta serve it is ready to be served.
  4. Additionally add cheese or pepper on top of it.
